How to Delete My Account

Last updated:

If you wish to delete your account with EliteKpJewellers, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Log In

Log in to your EliteKpJewellers account using your credentials.

Step 2: Navigate to Account Settings

Go to the 'Account Settings' section of the application.

Step 3: Request Account Deletion

In the Account Settings, you will find an option to 'Delete Account'. Click on this option.

Step 4: Confirm Deletion

You will be asked to confirm that you want to delete your account. Please note that deleting your account will result in the loss of all benefits and savings associated with your gold chit schemes.

Step 5: Contact Customer Support

If you encounter any issues or need further assistance, please contact our customer support at [Insert Contact Information].

Important Notes

Deleting your account is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, you will lose access to all the benefits and savings associated with your gold chit schemes.
